Brand Story

FHS is an upscale mens smart casual lifestyle brand offering an exciting range of merchandise. Adapting to the latest trends in the fashion industry, we have redefined the concept of a modern man and are ready to set new trends in smart casuals. The brand has created its own sense of style providing a wide variety in our product range. Furthermore, we tend to value diversity and strive to be bold, believe in creativity, and set our own trends in contemporary fashion. Since the mindset of a modern consumer is rapidly evolving, we envision ourselves to be timeless in our offerings not compromising on quality in any aspect.
